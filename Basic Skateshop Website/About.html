The Underground is every skater's favorite hangout spot for the central midwest skate scene. The Underground hosts live music, skate contests, skate camps and open skate sessions. The Underground is every skater's wet dream whether you love hitting the streets or riding some gnarly transition, we have something for just about everyone! The Underground is the largest indoor skatepark in Minnesota. Featuring a street course, a miniramp, a vert halfpipe and a concrete bowl. If that wasn't enough we also have a skate shop for all your skateboarding needs.

RULES

We get it rules suck but in order to ensure everyone has a good experience we have some ground rules!

  1. NO SCOOTERS, NO BIKES, NO BLADES, NO ROLLERSKATES. THIS IS A SKATBOARDING PARK NOT AN ACTION SPORTS PARK!
  2. RESPECT OTHER SKATERS AND FOLLOW BASIC PARK ETIQUETTE!
  3. PAY FOR YOUR SESSION OR YOU WILL BE BANNED!
  4. NO SMOKING OR VAPING INSIDE THE BUILDING!
  5. SKATERS UNDER THE AGE OF 16 MUST BE ACCOMPONIED BY AN ADULT!
